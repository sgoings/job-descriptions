## You Might Be

- A natural at aligning teams toward a common goal
- Great at fielding problems and directing energy toward solutions

## The Duties

1. Driven to build the strongest engineering team
2. Interested in gaining an expanded perspective in order to best determine direction
3. Love understanding what makes individuals tick
4. Energizing teams by connecting the "what" of engineering
